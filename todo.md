## What to focus on first

- For **SOC L1**, study: SIEM, logs, Windows/Linux basics, networking, phishing, malware, IDS/IPS, IOC/IOA, and incident response. [tryhackme](https://tryhackme.com/resources/blog/top-soc-analyst-interview-questions-for-2026)
- For **intermediate cyber security interviews**, study: encryption, MITM, hashing, access control, network security, DDoS, CSRF, threat modeling, and frameworks like MITRE ATT&CK. [guvi](https://www.guvi.in/blog/cybersecurity-interview-questions-and-answers/)
- For **scenario questions**, practice answering “What would you do first?” style questions with clear steps and escalation logic. [test-king](https://www.test-king.com/blog/soc-analyst-interview-preparation-key-questions-you-should-be-ready-for/)
